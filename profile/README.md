## Why doXmind? | 为什么选择 doXmind？

Writers juggle word processors, grammar checkers, and AI chatbots across multiple tabs. Context is lost at every switch. Most editors treat AI as a sidebar add-on — it doesn't understand your document, your style, or your intent.

doXmind is different. It's a single intelligent workspace where AI reads your entire document and presents every edit as an inline diff you accept or reject. You stay in control.

写作者在文字处理器、语法检查器、AI 聊天工具之间反复切换，每次切换都在丢失上下文。大多数编辑器把 AI 当作附属插件——它既不理解你的文档，也不了解你的风格和意图。

doXmind 不同。它是一个完整的智能工作空间：AI 阅读你的全文，以行内差异对比的形式呈现每一处修改，由你决定接受或拒绝。主导权始终在你手中。

## Core Features | 核心功能

### AI Autocomplete | AI 自动补全

Context-aware ghost text as you type. Two modes — **short** (word/phrase completion) and **long** (multi-sentence continuation triggered after headings, colons, and list markers). Press `Tab` to accept. Disabled in code blocks and tables to stay out of your way.

上下文感知的幽灵文本实时补全。两种模式——**短补全**（词/短语级）和**长补全**（在标题、冒号、列表标记后触发的多句续写）。按 `Tab` 采纳。在代码块和表格中自动禁用，绝不干扰。

### AI Chat with Document Tools | AI 对话 + 文档编辑

Chat with doXmind AI about your entire document. The AI doesn't just reply — it edits your document directly via tools, browses the web, and supports extended thinking for complex reasoning and vision for image analysis.

与 doXmind AI 围绕整篇文档对话。AI 不只是回复——它通过工具直接编辑文档、浏览网页，支持深度思考模式处理复杂推理，支持图片视觉分析。

### Knowledge Base | 知识库

Upload PDFs, DOCX, and PPTX as reference materials. The AI reads your uploaded documents, cites them in conversation, and grounds its suggestions in your own sources — not just its training data.

上传 PDF、DOCX、PPTX 作为参考资料。AI 阅读你上传的文档，在对话中引用它们，基于你的素材给出建议——而非仅依赖训练数据。

### Quick Edit | 快捷编辑

Select any text → right-click → choose an action: fix grammar, improve clarity, simplify, expand, adjust tone, translate. Edits are performed with full document context.

选中任意文本 → 右键 → 选择操作：修正语法、提升清晰度、精简、扩写、调整语气、翻译。所有编辑在完整文档上下文中执行。

### Diff Review | 差异审阅

Every AI edit appears as an inline diff. Navigate between changes with keyboard shortcuts. Accept or reject each change individually or all at once. A version snapshot is created automatically after acceptance.

每一处 AI 编辑都以行内差异形式呈现。用快捷键在变更间导航，逐条或批量接受/拒绝。接受后自动创建版本快照。

### Writing Review | 写作审阅

Full-document analysis across four dimensions: **correctness**, **clarity**, **tone**, and **engagement**. Each suggestion is categorized and can be accepted or dismissed individually.

从四个维度进行全文分析：**正确性**、**清晰度**、**语气**和**吸引力**。每条建议分类呈现，可逐条采纳或忽略。

### Presentation Mode | 演示模式

Turn any document into a full-screen slideshow — split by horizontal rules or headings. Supports light/dark themes, text alignment, keyboard navigation, and AI-powered slide summarization.

将文档一键转为全屏幻灯片——按分隔线或标题自动分页。支持明暗主题、文字对齐、键盘导航，以及 AI 驱动的幻灯片摘要。

### Version History | 版本历史

Timeline of every edit with type badges (manual / AI / quick-edit / restored). Preview any version, view diffs, and restore with one click.

以时间线展示每次编辑，标注类型（手动 / AI / 快捷编辑 / 恢复）。可预览任意版本、查看差异、一键恢复。

## What We Believe | 我们的理念

1. **Writer-first.** Every feature starts with: does this make writing easier? 每个功能的起点：这能让写作更轻松吗？
2. **AI as co-creator, not replacement.** AI amplifies your voice. Every edit is a suggestion you control. AI 放大你的声音，每一处修改都由你掌控。
3. **Your data stays yours.** We don't train on your content. 我们不会用你的内容进行训练。
4. **Simplicity by design.** No ribbon toolbars. No menu mazes. Tools appear when you need them. 无冗余工具栏，工具在你需要时自然出现。
