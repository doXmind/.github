## doXmind — AI-Native Writing Platform

**Write, research, and present — with AI built into every keystroke.**

doXmind is a distraction-free writing platform designed from the ground up with AI at its core. Not bolted on as a sidebar. Not hidden behind a chatbot. Woven into every part of how you write.

---

### The Problem

Writers today are scattered across too many tools — a word processor here, a grammar checker there, an AI chatbot in another tab. Context is lost at every switch. Flow is constantly interrupted.

Most editors treat AI as an afterthought. The AI doesn't understand your document, your style, or your research. And it forgets everything between sessions.

### Our Answer

**One intelligent workspace for the entire writing journey** — from research to drafting, revision to presentation.

- **AI Autocomplete** — Ghost text suggestions as you type. Press Tab to accept. Three modes: short, long, and adaptive.
- **AI Quick Edit** — Select any text to instantly fix grammar, improve clarity, simplify, expand, change tone, or translate.
- **AI Chat** — Have a contextual conversation with Claude about your entire document. Brainstorm, rewrite, and refine.
- **Diff Review** — Every AI edit appears as an inline diff. Accept or reject each change with one click. You stay in full control.
- **Knowledge Base** — Upload research papers, references, and notes. The AI reads them, cites them, and grounds every suggestion in your materials.
- **KB Agent** — Ask questions across all your documents. Get synthesized answers with source citations and relevance scores.
- **Semantic Search** — Find content by meaning, not just keywords.
- **Writing Review** — Comprehensive feedback on grammar, clarity, tone, and engagement — with categorized suggestions.
- **Presentation Mode** — Turn any document into a polished full-screen slideshow with a single click.

### What We Believe

1. **Writer-first, always.** Every feature starts with one question: does this make a writer's life easier?
2. **AI as partner, not replacement.** AI amplifies your voice. Every edit is a suggestion you control.
3. **Your data, your work.** We don't train on your content. Your writing stays private.
4. **Simplicity is a feature.** No ribbon toolbars. No menu mazes. Just a clean editor with tools that appear when you need them.
